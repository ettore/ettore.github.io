The Killing Moon

The Killing Moon is the first band I co-founded with Marco Antonelli. It existed approximately since 1994 until 2000. Main line-up:

  • Debora Stenta: vocals, guitar
  • Fabio Strada: guitar, accordion, vocals
  • Ettore Pasquini: keyboards, sequencer, programming
  • Marco Antonelli: bass
  • Mirco Foschi: drums

We appeared in some compilations and recorded the following:

The Killing Moon
First demo tape, 1995. Early-80s new wave vibes.
Slow Fog
Second demo. On some of the copies we distributed we added an additional song ("In a corner of this world"). Recorded during 1996.
Is dead (?)
The last EP. Recorded the 7th of February, 2000.
